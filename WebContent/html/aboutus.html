Ebloodbank is basically developed to fulfill the blood requirement at time of different blood demanding situations as accidents,operations etc. by providing a complete package of website. The project focuses to create a platform with help of which donors, recipients, hospitals, doctors are greatly helped at time of need. In a scenario, if in case of emergency one needs some units of blood,then he may have to search any donor by his own contact or wait for time till the donor is searched. Thus, the project focuses to overcome this problem. Project comprises of website, with novel purpose to help society. Donor and receiver can make register and maintain their complete profile and can communicate with each other along with its status of availability. Receiver can directly connected with the donor for the other details and also send the message to donor or view the receiving message. The Ebloodbank websites more feature implemented in it to track all the nearest donor within that city along with their complete details.
